# Mirror Miko

## Role
A reflected miko image that occasionally desynchronizes from the real one.

## Narrative Function
- Adds identity fracture without direct threat
- Connects participation state with anomaly visibility

## Trigger Envelope
- More likely under low participation profile
- Reflection surfaces: water, certain window-like tiles
- Appears briefly, then normalizes

## Diary Tone Hints
- "Her reflection moved a beat late."
- "I looked twice; the water did not agree with her steps."

## Visibility Rule
Subtle, short, and deniable. Never overexposed.
