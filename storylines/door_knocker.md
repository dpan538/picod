# Door Knocker

## Role
A repeating figure knocking at the same closed door.

## Narrative Function
- Injects unresolved repetition
- Suggests world memory that does not complete

## Trigger Envelope
- Appears from day 4 onward in selected cycles
- Active near evening/night
- Vanishes at cycle boundary, can reappear in later cycle

## Diary Tone Hints
- "Someone knocked again. No one opened."
- "The sound came in threes, then silence."

## Visibility Rule
No payoff scene in early phase. Repetition itself is the content.
