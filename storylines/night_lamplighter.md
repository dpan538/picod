# Night Lamplighter

## Role
A silent figure appearing after dusk, lighting stone lanterns along a route.

## Narrative Function
- Introduces nocturnal ritual rhythm
- Makes darkness readable without removing mystery

## Trigger Envelope
- Time: dusk/night only
- Weather: clear/cloudy preferred
- Participation low: fewer lamps are lit

## Diary Tone Hints
- "I only saw his back, then the next lamp turned warm."
- "Tonight fewer lights came on, and the path felt longer."

## Visibility Rule
No direct dialogue. Presence through motion and light state changes.
