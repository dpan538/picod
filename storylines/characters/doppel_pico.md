# Doppel Pico

## Core
A synchronized second Pico appears briefly, then disappears without log explanation.

## Presence
- Trigger suggestion: day 5+
- Lasts only a few seconds

## Dialogue Tone
- "For a breath, there were two of me with one heartbeat."
- "We stopped together and the wind paused too."
- "Then only one set of steps remained."
