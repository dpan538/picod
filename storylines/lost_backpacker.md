# Lost Backpacker

## Role
An external wanderer whose gear and behavior do not fit this world.

## Narrative Function
- Brings outside-world mismatch
- Creates cross-region movement and soft unease

## Trigger Envelope
- Appears from cycle 2+
- Roams edge and mid-path nodes
- Never finds a valid exit

## Diary Tone Hints
- "He unfolded a map, but nothing on it matched this place."
- "He asked no one, just kept circling the same crossing."

## Visibility Rule
No explicit exposition. The mismatch is shown, not explained.
