# Light Between Torii

## Role
A non-human glow appearing between torii markers.

## Narrative Function
- Binds cycle progression to visible anomaly
- Converts ceremonial structures into time instruments

## Trigger Envelope
- Requires at least 2 torii in world state
- Frequency scales with cycle depth
- Near cycle 7, silhouette becomes clearer

## Diary Tone Hints
- "Between the gates, a pale light held still for a breath."
- "Tonight the space between torii looked occupied." 

## Visibility Rule
Not treated as NPC. Rendered as presence, not character.
