# Umbrella Woman

## Role
A static watcher visible in rain or fog, usually at a peripheral line.

## Narrative Function
- Adds weather-bound apparition layer
- Reinforces quiet dread through stillness

## Trigger Envelope
- Weather: rain/fog only
- Early days: uncertain sightings
- Later cycles: clearer confirmation

## Diary Tone Hints
- "In the mist, I thought someone stood at the edge."
- "The umbrella never moved, but I felt watched." 

## Visibility Rule
She does not move, does not speak, does not approach.
